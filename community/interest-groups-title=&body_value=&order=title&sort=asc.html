Future of ShanghAI Lectures The ShanghAI Lectures are officially over now – but perhaps they should live on, in a new form. What are your suggestions for future editions of "ShanghAI Lectures" (or whatever they will be called)? Do you think we should stick to videoconferences, connecting entire lecture rooms? Or would you suggest another technology – for example, a virtual world (Wonderland, OpenQwaq, OpenSim, …)? Did you like the guest lectures, or would you prefer only the bare-bones "class" by Rolf Pfeifer? Maybe a more interactive seminar instead of lectures? Feel free to post your ideas and comments!
